Eyebrows

MICROBLADING / 3D

Microblading is the most natural looking permanent makeup technique available. Small, crisp , superficial incisions (hair strokes) are placed into the skin mimicking ones natural hair growth pattern. This technique is best suited for clients who have even hair growth throughout both brows. Microblading is not recommended for oily skin types.

OMBRE SHADING

Ombre shading is a light dusting of pigment throughout the front of the brow graduating in color depth towards the tails of the brow to create a soft, powder effect recreating the effect of filling them in with powder makeup.

POWDER BROW

Powdered brows are a bit more dramatic than ombre. It is used to recreate filling them in with pencil or a more dramatic brow style. Shading is done evenly throughout the entire brow.

COMBO-BROW

A combination brow is a combination of a powder or ombre brow and microblading. This technique is best suited for clients who have less hair towards the tail of the brow.

Microblading 3d process & aftercare

Microblading is a two step process. After 4 weeks your brows will have healed and we aim to work with a 60% retention rate. This is based upon our products, pigments and skill used in the initial treatment. If you were to leave your brows at this stage they would still look ok but you would not see the full benefit of a beautiful pair of brows for the full 12-18months. The initial top up at 4-6 weeks allows me to top up the strokes that need a touch up, bringing the pigment back up to 100%.

A few more things to consider BEFORE the microblading treatment, if you have any questions about any of it please just shout:

  • Do not drink coffee, alcohol or energizing drinks on the day of treatment.
  • Do not take Aspirin, Niacin, Vitamin E or Ibuprofen 24 hours before treatment.
  • Do not take omega3 (fish oil) 1 week before treatment.
  • Do not tan or have intense sun exposure 3 days before treatment.
  • No waxing 2-3 days before treatment.
  • No chemical peels, dermabrasion, laser or any other intense treatments like Botox 2 weeks before or after the treatment.
  • Skin should be healthy, non-irritated and blemish free. I cannot blade over spots or Moles.

A Typical Powder / Ombre / Combi brows treatment…

  1. Before the appointment begins, I will provide a quick consultation to discuss expectations and results of the treatment.
  2. I will then draw on the eyebrow shape so brows are measured perfectly to complement your unique face shape and size.
  3. Next, I will choose the perfect color.
  4. Initial strokes are placed, the first pass, then anesthetic is applied to reduce any discomfort.
  5. Pigment is worked into each stroke to define the brow. The Second Pass.
  6. After, a pigment mask is left to absorb into the skin.
  7. Once that’s finished, I will clean the area thoroughly.
  8. After the service, I will apply a balm and you will need to continue with aftercare which I will provide you with. I cannot guarantee your end result! I honestly wish I could , please read below. Do you have…

Very oily skin? Enlarged pores on your forehead/face or acne prone skin? Skin thats very sensitive or reactive? Eczema, psoriasis, rosacea or melasma? Really thick, dark, coarse eyebrow hair?

If you answered yes to any of these – unfortunately, brows will not last very long on you skin and details may fade faster. Please keep this in mind when deciding to get shading by me. You may need additional appointments and/or more frequent touch ups. A shaded brow may last longer and give better results, but results cannot be guaranteed!

Powder / Ombre / Combi brows Aftercare – Please follow!

The best thing to do is to not do too much! Please read through all of these aftercare instructions prior to booking to ensure you can commit to this treatment. Allowing yourself 7-10 days of healing time for your Brows will not do do almost nothing . To not use cream and let healedvyour brows naturaly by self.
